Z2Pack

Berryology and topology

General information

Code homepage: https://z2pack.greschd.ch/

Full documentation: https://z2pack.greschd.ch/

Source code: https://github.com/Z2PackDev/Z2Pack

Detailed information

Short description: Z2Pack automates the calculation of topological numbers of band-structures. It works with first-principles calculations (z2pack.fp), tight-binding models (z2pack.tb) and explicit Hamiltonian matrices (z2pack.hm).
